Description

It's a multiplayer, cooperative tower defense game with different possibles interactions between the players. You play as a builder who has the duty of kill dreams. As a builder, you have to build some construction who kills the around of it so, the player must know where to place and what to place, to counter the monsters who are willing to kill the dream.

History

Everyone blames the death for those who die, but have you ever think how hard is to work as death? Well, this game shows us that it isn't an easy work, it's hard, complicated and with a lots of unexpected problems.

Every dream that we dream, we create a possibility, with which we destroy. Every soul, every, creature, or even everyone that we create in our dream must be erased from the universe and this hard work is led by the only one capable of this hard work, the death.

After years of years in some delusional place, with the lonely itself, death is trying to build an army of dream destroyer, creatures that are capable of killing with such cold and hardness as death itself, but the dreamers became sleeper, delusional and depressingly lonely. So, death itself can't handle the job and is hiring.
